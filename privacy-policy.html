STORE LYFT PRIVACY POLICY

At StoreLyft ("we," "us," or "our"), we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our ecommerce platform and related services ("Services"), including when you:

  • Visit our website at storelyft.com
  • Make purchases through our platform
  • Contact our customer support team

KEY POINTS

What information we collect: When you use StoreLyft, we may collect your name, contact details, payment information, and data about how you interact with our platform.

How we use your information: We use your data to process orders, improve our services, communicate with you, and protect against fraud.

Your rights: You may request access to your information or ask us to update or delete it by contacting us at privacy@storelyft.com.

1. INFORMATION COLLECTION

Information You Provide

When you create an account or make purchases with StoreLyft, we collect:

  • Full name and contact information
  • Shipping and billing addresses
  • Payment details
  • Purchase history

Automated Collection

Our systems automatically gather:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and location data
  • Usage patterns and preferences

2. USE OF INFORMATION

We process your data to:

  • Complete and ship your orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our ecommerce platform
  • Send service-related communications
  • Prevent fraudulent activity

3. DATA SHARING

We may share information with:

  • Payment processors to complete transactions
  • Shipping carriers to deliver orders
  • Service providers who assist our operations

4. DATA SECURITY

StoreLyft implements industry-standard measures to protect your information, though no system can guarantee absolute security.

5. YOUR CHOICES

You can:

  • Update your account information at any time
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your personal data

6. POLICY UPDATES

We may occasionally update this policy. Continued use of StoreLyft after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
