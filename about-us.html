About StoreLyft

StoreLyft is a specialized lab founded by Rohit Manna, a conversion strategist with a passion for transforming ordinary Shopify stores into high-performance sales machines. We don't just redesign - we engineer how your brand sells.

Our approach is rooted in the belief that every store should be both sleek in design and engineered for maximum conversions. We combine elegant aesthetics with conversion science to deliver measurable performance that drives real results.

Built for Growth. Designed for Clarity.

We started as small business owners navigating the shift to digital. What we found was fragmentation—juggling multiple agencies, misaligned visions, and scattered communication. So we created StoreLyft: a conversion-focused, design-led solution that brings every essential under one roof. For brands that are ready to scale with elegance and precision.
